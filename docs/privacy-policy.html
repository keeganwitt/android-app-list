Our privacy policy is very straight-forward.

  • We don't collect your data, with the exception of crash reports.
  • We don't share your data with anyone else, with the exception of crash reports.

This app uses Firebase Crashlytics to automatically report crashes. This data is used to help fix bugs and improve the app. The information collected does not personally identify you. For more details on the data collected, please see the Firebase privacy policy.

You may opt out of sharing crash reports. In that case, no data is collected or shared.
